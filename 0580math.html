IGCSE (0580) Mathematics [Extended]

There are a variety of Mathmatics codes when it comes to CAIE, but this is the most common one as this does not include coursework. This website only includes Extended papers rather than Core. Most textbooks differ every year so we selected the two common ones. As of 2025, CAIE has two papers for extended. Paper 2 (Non-Calculator) and Paper 4 (Calculator) will have a combined total of 200 marks, eaching being worth 100 marks each.
