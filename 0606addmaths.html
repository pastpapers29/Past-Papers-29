IGCSE (0606) Additional Mathematics

The Additional Mathematics IGCSE from Cambridge is considered the hardest CAIE subject as it includes a variety of topics that are usually covered in A Level. Its content includes some topics from 0580, like Functions and Trigonometry, but goes further in depth, adding other concepts like Domain and Range, Integration, and Trig Identities. Papers 1 and 2 have a combined total of 160 marks, each being worth 80 marks respectively.
