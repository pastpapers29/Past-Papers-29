IGCSE (0460) Geography

Geography IGCSE is one that is extremely heavy with content, along with a lot of case studies to memorise. As of 2027 onwards, the papers will change drastically, with Papers 1 and 2 being split by the types of geography. Calculators are allowed in all papers.
