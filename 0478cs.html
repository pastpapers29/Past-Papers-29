IGCSE (0478) Computer Science

The Computer Science IGCSE consists of 10 chapters based on the syllabus. Chapters 1 to 6 would be tested in Paper 1 whilst Chapters 7 to 10 would be tested on Paper 2. Both papers are 75 marks each, bringing the overall total to 150 marks. Calculators are not allowed in both papers.
